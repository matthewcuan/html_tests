I Wrote a Simple Song

By Billy Preston

  • A song by American soul musician Billy Preston
  • First released as the title track to his album of the same name in November 1971
  • Written by Preston and his regular collaborator, Joe Greene
  • Peaked at number 77 on the Billboard Hot 100
  • The recording features a guest appearance from George Harrison and Quincy Jones
